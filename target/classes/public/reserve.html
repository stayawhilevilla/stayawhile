RULES

b. No third-party bookings. You can not assign this agreement to another person. We will only check-in the person named on this agreement.

c. After hour check-ins. We consider any check-in after 6pm (PST) to be an after hour check-in. After hour check-in must be approved in writing by Stay Awhile. Will not be responsible for any delays. If you do not arrive within a 30 minute window on an approved after hours check-in will not check you in until at the following day at 9am.

d. People other than those in the Guest party set forth above may not stay overnight in the property. Any other person in the property is the sole responsibility of Guest. Visitors are not allowed unless approved in advance by Stay Awhile Villas.

e. The owner is not responsible for any accidents, injuries or illness that occurs while on the premises or its facilities. The Homeowners are not responsible for the loss of personal belongings or valuables of the guest. By accepting this reservation, it is agreed that all guests are expressly assuming the risk of any harm arising from their use of the premises or others whom they invite to use the premise.

f. Keep the property and all furnishings in good order. Guests may not move furniture around.

g. Only use appliances for their intended uses.

h. Pets are not allowed unless approved in writing by Stay Awhile Villas.

i. Parking: Vehicles are to be parked in designated parking areas only. Any illegally parked cars are subject to towing; applicable fines/towing fees are the sole responsibility of the vehicle owner.

j. Housekeeping: Available upon request for an additional fee.

k. Outdoor Fire-pit: The fire-pit is natural gas. Please do not throw anything or other combustible materials in the fire-pit. Do not use fire-pits around children.

l. Pool: Any guests who are unable to swim must be attended and supervised by a capable swimmer. If guests wish for pool safety equipment they must notify Stay Awhile Villas at the time of booking. Always use caution when near a pool or body of water.

m. Umbrellas: Must be closed properly when not in use. If it is windy outside please close them. If they are left open and brake guest will be responsible for replacement of entire cost of umbrella.

n. Missing Garage remotes and keys will result in a $100 replacement fee per remote and or per key.

o. Only flush the normal use of toilet paper. Do not flush any feminine products, disposal wipes or trash down the toilet. Guest will be responsible for the cost to get the lines snaked.

p. Housekeeping: If the home is not left in the reasonable manner additional housekeeping fees will apply.

q. Golf Cart: If you are renting a property that comes with a Golf Cart you must supply a valid drivers license and car insurance. You must adhere to all rules and will be asked to sign supplemental terms.

r. Professional filming or photography is strictly prohibited. If you are caught filming or using the property for professional purposes Stay Awhile will collect filming fees and you will be asked to vacate the premises.

s. No lifeguard on duty: whether you are staying in a home with a pool or a beachfront property we do not have a lifeguard on duty and you are responsible for any incapable swimmer(s).
